Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with:

Data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We implement appropriate safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, though no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact support@cube.app to exercise these rights.

7. Cookie Consent

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enable essential functionality (authentication, security).

  • Personalize content and ads (with your consent).

Upon first visit, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept all, reject non-essential, or customize preferences. You can withdraw consent or change settings at any time via the cookie management tool in your account settings.
